Professor Dataset Analysis

This dataset published by Prof. Huang in Brown University. It contains information of over 2,000 computer science professors at top universitiesm, which include the names, institution, degrees obtained, and when they joined the university, for professors in the traditional role that involves both research and teaching.

Where do the professors study for their Ph.D. degree?

The schools are ranked by their ranking. As we can see from the following heat map, dark color cells are concentrated around the upper-left corner, top few rows and diagonal. That means best cs schools graduate more phds that end up in a top 50 institute, and a phd in top universities usually become a professor in a top university. The dark color in the diagonal means there is a significant portion of phd stayed in their own university as a professor



Which school has cultivated the most professors?

The following pie chart shows the number of Ph.D graduate for each school that end up being a professor in a top 50 cs university. It's not suprise that MIT, Berkeley, Stanford and CMU took the top spot as they have large cs departments and the phd students are excellent



The following tree map will give you more detailed information of the distribution of the sub-fields of Ph.D. graduates for each school.



Which school will you teach at?

You can select your Bachelors school, Doctorate school and your sub-field. We will predict the possible university that you will teach at in the future using Naive Bayes Networks based on the professor dataset.
